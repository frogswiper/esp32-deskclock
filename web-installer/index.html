Install DeskClock

Flash firmware directly from your browser. No drivers, no IDE required. Works on Chrome and Edge.

⚠ Web Serial is not supported in this browser. Please use Chrome or Edge. ⚠ This page must be served over HTTPS (or localhost) to use Web Serial.

What happens next

1
Connect your device Plug the JC4827W543 into USB, click Install, select the COM port.
2
Firmware is flashed Takes ~30 seconds. The device will restart automatically.
3
Open the Settings tab Tap the ⚙ icon at the bottom of the screen. Enter your WiFi name, password, and city.
4
Tap Connect & Save The clock connects to WiFi, syncs the time, and fetches weather automatically. No account or API key needed.
5
Done! Clock, today's weather, and a 7-day forecast — all on the screen.
